There is nothing purer than honesty.
Nothing sweeter than charity.
Nothing warmer than love,
Nothing brighter than virture 
and nothing more steadfast than 
faith 

These united in in one mind, form 
the purest, the sweetest, the richest
the brightest and the most steadfast 
happiness

A. Williams 
Forresters Terrace
Ruabon
At 
Hampton HAll
May 5th 1918
