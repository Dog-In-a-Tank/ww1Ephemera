Ma freen', ye bade me write a line 
inta yer Auto Bookie, 
am puzzled what tae scribble doon, 
Tae interest an suit ye 
The less I say perhaps tis best 
for oft ma toungue an pen slip 
But, Let me frae ma hieland heart
aye promise ye true friendship 

Driver D Murray 
533 Coy A.S.C. 
27/2/17
at Upper Hatton Dunkeld
