On land & sea, as brothers we;
United true, shall dauntless stand,
Our "Motto" be "'Brittannia free,"
True sons of Empire in Hand

--

Though battered & torn. furrowed and worn
fretted by time - well: ne'er forget
ever we hail it, never well fail it
Emblem, of "honour and freedom yet 

M M
31st Jan 1917
