The Kaisers letter to King George.
Potsdam 
Berlin.


Mine dear Cousin,
	How vos you going along? How you vos?
Do not nom to see me for I like not your ships.
I vos kom to see you if you move dem ships away
Do not be frightened, I kom for your goots!

	Vot i kom for? Vot I vanted ? I vants 
der leedle Bank von England for mine Frau. I 
vants der dockyards, mein gott! Take dem Ships 
away and I kom at once. I wants der leedle Isle 
von Wight and her luffly Cows ver I shall hold vun
big regatta. 

	I vants der leedle places, India, Canadas, 
australias, for mine sohns. I had seven (Hoch der
Iron Prinz), and der each vants von ver he can sit
in de sun and eat his Lieber Wurst and trink his laager.

Gott im Himmel you have much more dan is good for you
mine dear cousin. But Ireland I cants not. ho! 
Der Teufel! 

Hearty Greeting from
Wilhelm.
P.S. move dem ships away or I get angry and Kom not

R B Phillips
