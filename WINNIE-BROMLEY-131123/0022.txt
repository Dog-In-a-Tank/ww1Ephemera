EXPLANATION (MUCH NEEDED.)
THIS PICTURE WIEN YOUNG WAS MEANT TO GROW UP
 QUITE NICELY. HAD IT BEEN TAKEN CARE OF IN IS EARLY
 MOMENTS IT MIGHT HAVE BECOME " THE MINARET " BUT
IT HAS NOW & FOR EVER BECOME "THE DAUB"

AMPONE WISHNG TO VIEN "THE DAUB"
IS RECOMMENDED TO STAND AT LEAST FOUR YARDS
AWAY - CLOSE HIS EYES + LOOK INTENTLY. IT WILL THEN 
APPEAR IN GOOD COLOURS.

GEP
Will regret that two pages of this
valuable book have been pinched.

1927.
